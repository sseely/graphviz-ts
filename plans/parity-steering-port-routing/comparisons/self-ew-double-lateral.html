Diagnosis

The loop shape was always faithful (lateral double-bulge, vertical extent to y=-144). The whole node-A region used to translate ~5.5pt in x — but that was the drawing bbox, not a width reservation.

The lateral loop bulges left of node A (to x≈-5.4). The old control-hull updateBbBz over-reserved bb on the right by the control-point overshoot, shifting the graph transform. The faithful refined-curve bb (emit.c:746) reserves exactly what dot does, so the node region and the incident A->B edge now sit where dot puts them. viewBox 85x152 matches dot exactly.

How it was fixed

The selfRightSpace reservation (AD5-frozen splines-selfedge.ts) was never the cause — the divergence was the bb. Pinned as a dot-oracle in steering-port-regression.test.ts.
