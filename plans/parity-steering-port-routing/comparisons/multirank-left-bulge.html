Diagnosis

dot’s loop swings to x=-21 (left of the origin); TS clamps the left excursion at x=0 and the whole loop translates right. The deep 4-rank case shows the same (~11pt).

The faithful chain assembly is correct for loops within the drawing bounds; the left-extent of the assembled corridor (computeLeftBound / maximal_bbox clamp for the virtual nodes) does not reach as far negative as dot’s. Not a Proutespline numeric divergence (Y and shape are faithful). The straight-mode run optimization is also unported.

Why excluded (not fixed)

The named steering cases (TOP, RIGHT-lateral, ≤3 ranks) match within 0.32pt and are pinned. Left-extent + straight-mode are larger corridor-assembly changes.
